Questions

Can't I just use ChatGPT for this?

You can. ChatGPT gives you 5–10 generic titles with no context. TitleForge filters by your specific medium — a book title and a blog title need different things — scores each title's appeal so you're not guessing, and generates 100 at once instead of 10. It's the difference between a generalist and a specialist.

Is $19/month worth it for titles?

If you publish once a month, one better title can be the difference between someone clicking through or scrolling past. At $19/month, that's about 63 cents a day. Cancel with one click if it doesn't earn its keep — no contracts, no lock-in.

How does the appeal score work?

Each title is rated on a 0–100 scale by AI, based on patterns from successful titles in that category and genre. It's an AI-estimated appeal score — a heuristic, not a live poll. It helps you quickly spot which titles have the most pull.

What does the title breakdown analysis show?

Below each title, you'll find its breakdown analysis showing: Curiosity gap (how much it makes you want to know more), Emotional trigger (the emotion it evokes), Power words (impactful words detected), Length analysis (whether it's optimal for the medium), and Specificity (how concrete or abstract it is). Hover or click the ? icon next to each metric for a quick explanation. Full breakdown values are available on Pro only — free users can see which metrics exist to know what they're missing.

What's the difference between the web app and the desktop app?

The web app generates titles using AI in your browser — it's great for quick sessions and works on any device. The desktop app is a downloadable program that works entirely offline using a curated title database. No internet needed, no monthly fees. Web Pro subscribers get the desktop Basic license for free.

What happens to my data and privacy?

Your titles, history, and favorites are stored securely in your account. We don't sell your data, we don't train AI on your titles, and you can export or delete everything anytime. TitleForge is a tool you use — not a data harvesting operation.

Do I need an account?

No — you can try TitleForge as a guest with 3 free generations, no signup. To get 5 generations per day, save your history, and access the dashboard, create a free account. To unlock all features, upgrade to Pro.
