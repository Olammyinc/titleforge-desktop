What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Basic has the full offline engine, all 16 categories, and up to 25 titles per generation. Pro adds up to 100 titles per generation and the ability to bring your own AI key (OpenAI, DeepSeek, Claude, Gemini) for cloud-powered titles when you want them.

Do I need an internet connection to use TitleSmith?

No. The core engine — title generation, dashboard, history, projects — works completely offline. You only need internet if you choose to use the optional AI key feature in the Pro version.

Which platforms does it run on?

Windows, macOS, and Linux. One purchase works on all three.

Is this a subscription?

No. You pay once — $29 or $49 — and you own TitleSmith forever. Free updates for the lifetime of the product.
